## What the Leagle API Actually Does
The API delivers clinical-grade regulatory intelligence via several mission-critical endpoints:
1.  **`neural_search`**: Performs high-speed semantic retrieval across millions of legal documents. It doesn't just look for words; it understands the *intent* of the query.
2.  **`key_management`**: A full lifecycle suite for programmatic credentials, allowing companies to rotate secrets and manage institutional sub-keys.
3.  **`institutional_regulations`**: Provides direct access to a curated, real-time feed of government and regulatory mandates.
4.  **`audit_heartbeat`**: Returns a signed JSON proof of service, usable in insurance claims or legal audits to prove compliance was searched at a specific timestamp.
